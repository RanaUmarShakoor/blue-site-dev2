"Full IT Stack"

ICT Service Desk - MBO

An IT service desk employee provides technical support to users and registers incidents. They coordinate and guide users in IT use and ensure satisfaction through follow-up of outstanding cases. In short, essential for a well-functioning IT service within an organization.

ICT Service Administrator - MBO

An ICT administrator manages an organization's IT infrastructure, including networks and servers. They provide technical support, ensure security against cyberthreats, manage software licenses, implement backups and keep abreast of technological developments for strategic decisions.

Data Scientist - MBO & HBO

A Data Scientist collects, cleans and analyzes data from various sources. They use advanced analytics and data visualization to discover trends and gain insight. They predict trends and provide valuable information. They support data-driven decisions within an organization.

Software Developer - MBO & HBO

A Software Developer designs, develops, implements and maintains software applications. Cooperation and documentation are important for the quality and maintainability of the software. In short, they deliver high-quality software solutions that meet customer and user needs.

ICT Consultant - HBO

An IT consultant advises organizations on IT strategy and infrastructure. They analyze current systems, design solutions and supervise implementations of new technologies. They offer tailor-made solutions and support organizations in the effective use of technology.

Database Specialist - MBO & HBO

We often store data used by an application in a database. A database specialist, or database administrator, designs, implements, manages and secures databases. Their role is critical to the integrity and availability of data within the organization.

Management - HBO

An Operations Manager manages day-to-day IT operations and optimizes processes to increase productivity, including budget and resource management. A Project Manager plans, coordinates and leads specific IT projects, sets goals, manages resources and communicates with stakeholders for successful project delivery.

Business Analyst - HBO

A Business Process Analyst is crucial for analyzing, modeling and optimizing business processes to improve efficiency and productivity. They identify bottlenecks, collect requirements and suggest improvements. Stakeholder collaboration and report generation drive sustainable improvements within an organization.
